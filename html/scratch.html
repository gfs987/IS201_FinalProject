Tips

Here are some top tips on baking:

  1. Be time conscious
    • The only thing worse than underbaked bread is burned bread. Don't lose track of how long it's been in the oven.
    • Always read your recipe through before starting. Some recipes include a "let the dough rise overnight" step, which could ruin your plans!
  2. Prepare everything in advance
    • Bakers are most successful when everything is measured in advance. Give it a try!
    • A clean kitchen is a happy kitchen, and a happy baker needs a happy kitchen. Don't let things get messy before or after your bake.
  3. Always follow the recipe... unless you don't want to
    • You'd be surprised by how many recipes are written by well-intentioned but inexperienced authors. If your mom does it differently, you should too!
    • Feel free to make additions for flavor, but try not to add too many things that change the consistency or feel of your dough/batter. This affects baking time and temperature.
    • Never make substitutions that make no sense. Even if someone online says you can substitute applesauce for eggs, that doesn't mean you should try making custard tarts with applesauce instead of eggs.
